Adrians bio:   Adrian has been studying massage & yoga passionately since 2004.  He chose one of the best massage schools in the world,  Maui School of Therapuetic Massage, graduating in September 2010 where he honed his skills in Lomi Lomi, Swedish, Sports Massage, NMT, Deep Tissue, and more.  Upon request he can also introduce sound therapy with Tibetan Healing bowls, and a high end Tactile Sound Healing table.  He spent 9 months in India studying at various yoga schools, ashrams & meditations centers including a 2 month yoga intensive at Agama Yoga in Rishikesh, India followed by a 500 hours/3 month teachers training at their mother school in Koh Phangan, Thailand.  He prefers to teach meditation at the moment but can do private yoga classes upon request.

He continues his education constantly learning to help people lighten up & ENLIGHTEN UP with Yoga, Massage, Meditation & Sound Healing.
